Our Story

A Corner of Calm
in a Busy City

Ember & Salt was born from a simple belief: food should feel like coming home. Nestled in the heart of Greenfield City, we source local ingredients, roast our own beans, and bake everything fresh each morning.

From the first espresso pulled at 7 AM to the last slice of cake at closing, every detail is intentional — because you deserve more than just a meal.
